# PDB 6acd

## Overview

**Protein name**: surface glycoprotein

**Organism**: SARS-CoV

**Method**: Cryo-EM

## Description

Trypsin-cleaved and low pH-treated SARS-CoV spike glycoprotein with one RBD in up conformation. Part of a series of studies on the conformational landscape of spike in complex with ACE2
