This folder contains AUSPEX results. These are results of the analysis of deposited diffraction data with [AUSPEX](www.auspex.de). AUSPEX can help to detect problems in experimental setup, integration, scaling and conversion from intensities to amplitudes. More infos at auspex.de

Files:

<pdbid>-sf.mtz - mtz file generated from ../../<pdbid>-sf.cif with CCP4 cif2mtz

*.jpg - Pictures of different scatter plots of diffraction data, depending on the type of diffraction data deposited (intensities and/or amplitude). For examples and explanations, go to auspex.de.
	

AUSPEX: Thorn, A., Parkhurst, J.M., Emsley, P., Nicholls, R., Evans, G., Vollmar, M., Murshudov, G.N. (2017) AUSPEX: a graphical tool for X-ray diffraction data analysis, Acta Cryst D73, 729-737.

CIF2MTZ: http://www.ccp4.ac.uk/html/cif2mtz.html
